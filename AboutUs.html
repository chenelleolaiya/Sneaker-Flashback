About Us

Chenelle 23 (left) a Law graduate and Liane 22 (right) an Ancient History graduate, have known each other for 3 years.They met at university and both share a strong passion for everything fashion related. Liane & Chenelle are both self-confessed sneakerheads which is why they have created an encyclopedia of different Nike sneakers and models.

With music acting as the soundtrack to the history of sneakers and fashion trends having influenced sneaker designs since the Nike waffle sole first touched the ground in the 1970s, it is clear that these cultural elements are all intertwined.

Through music reviews, the latest trends and sneaker releases, Sneaker Flashback lets you kick back and be a part of the sneaker movement that brings together all these important cultural elements.

So tie up your laces and join the Sneaker Flashback mission that not only aims to align itself with the cultural urban movement of fashion, music and all things sneakers but to define it.
